Slay the Spire Stats | Web Application

Slay the Spire (StS) Stats is a personalized platform I created to track and analyze my progress in my favorite video game, Slay the Spire. I am collecting and cleaning my gameplay data and I have designed an interactive dashboard that allows me to visualize key metrics and performance over time. The site enables me to easily track my stats and optimize my future gameplay strategies. With custom visualizations and interactive features, the website not only showcases my technical skills but also serves as a valuable tool for enhancing my gaming experience.
